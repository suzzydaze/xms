GUIDE

Buying a CPU? (the “brain” of your gaming PC) can be exciting—but it’s super important to stay safe and avoid scams. Here’s a simple safety guide for teens in NZ buying new or used CPUs locally, nationally, or even internationally.

Mid-Range CPUs: Intel Core i5-12400F / AMD Ryzen 5 5600X

These CPUs offer excellent performance for gamers and everyday users without breaking the bank. They are ideal for mainstream builds focused on performance per dollar.

  • Intel Core i5-12400F: 6 cores and 12 threads, great for gaming and multitasking. No integrated graphics, making it best suited for systems with dedicated GPUs.
  • AMD Ryzen 5 5600X: 6 cores and 12 threads, known for strong single-thread performance and power efficiency, ideal for both gaming and productivity.
  • Target Users: Gamers, students, and professionals seeking a solid all-around experience at a mid-level price.

Upper Mid-Range CPUs: Intel Core i5-13600KF / AMD Ryzen 5 7600X

These CPUs represent a higher tier of performance for power users who want strong multitasking, streaming, and gaming capabilities.

  • Intel Core i5-13600KF: 14 cores (6 P-cores + 8 E-cores) and 20 threads, making it highly versatile for productivity and demanding workloads. No integrated GPU.
  • AMD Ryzen 5 7600X: 6 cores and 12 threads on AMD’s Zen 4 architecture, supporting DDR5 and PCIe 5.0 for future-ready systems.
  • Target Users: Content creators, gamers, and multitaskers looking for high performance with next-gen support.

High-End CPUs: Intel Core i9-13900K / AMD Ryzen 9 7950X

These flagship processors are designed for enthusiasts and professionals who need top-tier power for the most demanding workloads.

  • Intel Core i9-13900K: 24 cores (8 P-cores + 16 E-cores) and 32 threads, delivering extreme performance for gaming, streaming, 3D rendering, and more.
  • AMD Ryzen 9 7950X: 16 cores and 32 threads, based on AMD’s advanced 5nm Zen 4 architecture, with support for cutting-edge technologies like DDR5 and PCIe 5.0.
  • Target Users: Professional creators, overclockers, and enthusiasts who require maximum performance and future-proofing.
